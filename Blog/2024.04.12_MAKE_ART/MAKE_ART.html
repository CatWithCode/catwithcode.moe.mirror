Art is NOT Content!

Art and every kind of creation, especially in digital media, get's called content nowadays. From paintings to games to music, from videos to even books. How much, how long, how good? that is all people talk about. You could say it is just a word, but it represents a deeper issue with the way people look at art today: it is, for most, mindless stimulation. It is content.

I will leave the essays to the writers out there, but what I will do is fight against content and fight for my art. Whether it's code, programming, 3D modelling, game development, writing, reviewing, building things out of wood, drawing, ... or a blog it is ART. Everything we create with passion and love is art. Call me prentatios or think what I am saying is "cringe". I love what I do and I fight for it!

Stop using platforms and systems that enforce contentification.
