New Design!

Not much to say. I saw a font in a game, thought it looked really cool and after reading the licence I wanted to use it for my website. All resources can be found in the sources section. Thanks to "Abbie Gonzalez" for creating the beautiful and very readable "OpenDyslexic"-Font!

I also finally fixed my text block problem, where every part of the site was using headers for text. The old font made everything so small that I thought it was a good solution, but now it is much better to do it the "right" way.
